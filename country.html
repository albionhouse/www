Acknowledgement of Country

Always was and always will be Aboriginal Land.

In the spirit of reconciliation, Albion House Management acknowledges the Gadigal people of the Eora nation as the Traditional Owners of Country and custodians of the land on which the boarding house stands.  Albion House pays respect to their Elders, past and present, and extends that respect to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.

ALBION HOUSE is a registered general boarding house under the Boarding Houses Act 2012
