Albion House is a registered general boarding house under the NSW Boarding Houses Act 2012.

Albion House provides beds for a fee, for 5 or more residents, having private rooms with some common areas and shared facilities.

In the spirit of reconciliation, Albion House Management acknowledges the Gadigal people of the Eora nation as the Traditional Owners of Country and custodians of the land on which Albion House stands. Albion House pays respect to their Elders, past and present and extends that respect to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.
